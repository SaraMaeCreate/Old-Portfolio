Spring Hill Effort

The Spring Hill Effort was a collaboritory group project with Brianna Ricciardone as project manager, Alexandra Butera, Erin Kaminski, Samantha Mairson and I.
Our clients were a part of both the Digital Media Design Department at UCONN and also the Homer Babbidge Library looking to create a collaborative space within the library for scholars who would like to start publishing digitally.
The final deliverable of this project consisted of creating a brand identity, personas, research of similar efforts and logos.
